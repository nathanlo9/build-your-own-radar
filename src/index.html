Quadrants

  • Tools: Technologies such as frameworks, libraries, and utilities used to build and operate systems.
  • Techniques: Practices, patterns, and methodologies that guide how work is done.
  • Solutions: Internally-developed systems and services.
  • Platforms: Foundational environments or ecosystems that we build solutions on top of.

Rings

  • Adopt: Items that are mature and recommended for widespread use.
  • Assess: Items that are recommended but not mandatory; teams may experiment with them to determine fit and value.
  • Contain: Items that are no longer strategic and should not be adopted for new solutions; usage should be limited and phased out.
  • End-of-Life: Items no longer supported or approved for use as of a given date; teams should plan for decommissioning or replacement.
